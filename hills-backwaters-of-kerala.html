Hills & Backwaters of Kerala

4 Days / 3 Nights
Day 1 – Arrival in Cochin

Drive to the Enchanting
Hills of Munnar

Welcome to God’s Own Country! Upon arrival at Cochin Airport / Ernakulam Railway Station, our representative will warmly greet you and assist with your transfer to Munnar (approx. 4 hours) — a hill station draped in mist and endless tea gardens.

En route, admire the cascading Cheeyappara and Valara Waterfalls, spice plantations, and the scenic beauty of the Western Ghats.

On arrival, check into your Munnar resort, freshen up, and relax. In the evening, stroll through Munnar town or enjoy a peaceful walk amidst tea-scented hills.

✨ Overnight stay at Munnar.
Day 2 – Munnar Sightseeing

A Day in the Tea Gardens

After a hearty breakfast, set out for a full-day sightseeing tour of Munnar, one of South India’s most beautiful hill stations.

Visit the Eravikulam National Park (Rajamalai), home to the rare Nilgiri Tahr, and enjoy panoramic views of the tea-carpeted slopes. Later, visit Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and Kundala Lake, where you can enjoy boating amidst mist-covered hills. Don’t miss a stop at the Tea Museum, where you’ll learn how fresh tea leaves are transformed into the aromatic blends Munnar is famous for.

Evening free for leisure and shopping for local tea, spices, and handmade chocolates.

✨ Overnight stay at Munnar.
Day 3 – Munnar to Alleppey

Backwater Houseboat Experience

After breakfast, check out and drive to Alleppey (approx. 5 hours) — the Venice of the East. On arrival, board your traditional Kerala houseboat by 1:00 PM for an unforgettable backwater cruise through the tranquil canals of Vembanad Lake.

As your houseboat glides gently over the serene waters, admire the picturesque sights of lush paddy fields, coconut groves, village life, and charming houseboat docks. Enjoy a delicious Kerala-style lunch and dinner served onboard while relaxing in the lap of nature.

As the sun sets, the houseboat anchors for the night under a canopy of stars.

✨ Overnight stay on the Houseboat.
Day 4 – Alleppey to Cochin

Departure

Wake up to the soothing sounds of rippling water and chirping birds. After breakfast, your houseboat will cruise back to Alleppey Jetty by 9:00 AM. Disembark and drive back to Cochin, where you’ll be dropped at the Airport / Railway Station for your onward journey.

End of Tour
