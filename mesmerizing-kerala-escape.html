Mesmerizing Kerala Escape

5 Days / 4 Nights
Day 1 – Arrival in Cochin

Journey to the Enchanting
Hills of Munnar

Welcome to God’s Own Country! Upon your arrival at Cochin Airport / Railway Station, our warm and friendly representative will greet you and assist with your comfortable transfer to Munnar (approx. 4 hrs).

As you ascend the misty hills, let your senses be captivated by rolling tea gardens, sparkling waterfalls, and the fresh scent of mountain air — nature’s own grand welcome to Kerala’s most romantic hill station.

Check in to your charming Munnar resort, where tranquility meets luxury. Later, visit the Tata Tea Museum to discover the art of tea-making and savor a refreshing cup straight from the source.

As evening falls, wander through Munnar town, shop for fragrant spices, handmade chocolates, and local crafts — or simply unwind and breathe in the serene mountain mist.

✨ Overnight stay at Munnar.
Day 2

Exploring the Magical Munnar

Wake up to birdsong and a refreshing breeze. After breakfast, set out on a full-day exploration of Munnar’s natural treasures.

Visit the Eravikulam National Park, home to the rare Nilgiri Tahr, and enjoy panoramic views of lush green valleys. Continue to Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, Kundala Lake, and the vibrant Blossom Garden.

Don’t miss Top Station, where the clouds drift so close you can almost touch them — offering breathtaking views of the Western Ghats and beyond.

Spend the evening at leisure, perhaps sipping local tea at a cozy café or enjoying the golden hues of sunset.

✨ Overnight stay at Munnar.
Day 3 – Munnar to Thekkady

From the Hills to the Wild

After breakfast, bid farewell to the hills and drive to Thekkady (approx. 4 hrs) — Kerala’s wildlife haven surrounded by lush rainforests and spice plantations.

Upon arrival, check into your nature resort and relax amidst the soothing sounds of the jungle. Later, embark on a boat cruise across Periyar Lake, where you might spot elephants, bison, deer, and exotic birds along the forested banks.

Adventure seekers can indulge in optional activities such as Nature Walks, Bamboo Rafting, or the thrilling Periyar Tiger Trail organized by the Forest Department.

As night falls, enjoy a cultural Kathakali or Martial Arts show, or simply unwind in the aromatic spice-scented air.

✨ Overnight stay at Thekkady.
Day 4 – Thekkady to Alleppey

Backwater Bliss

After breakfast, journey to Alleppey (approx. 5 hrs) — lovingly called The Venice of the East.

By noon, step aboard your elegant private houseboat and set sail through Kerala’s enchanting backwaters. Drift along tranquil canals lined with coconut palms, spot local fishermen at work, and wave to smiling villagers as you glide past lush paddy fields.

Savor authentic Kerala cuisine prepared by your onboard chef — fragrant curries, crisp banana chips, and sweet payasam.

As the sun sets in a golden glow over Vembanad Lake, your houseboat anchors for the night. The silence of the backwaters, the shimmer of stars, and the gentle ripple of water create a truly magical atmosphere.

✨ Overnight stay on the Houseboat.
Day 5 – Alleppey to Cochin Departure

Farewell to God’s Own Country

Wake up to the melody of birds and the rhythmic lapping of water. After breakfast, your houseboat will return to Alleppey Jetty by 9:00 a.m.

Bid adieu to the serene backwaters and drive back to Cochin, cherishing the timeless memories of misty hills, fragrant spice gardens, and shimmering lagoons.

End of Tour

Until We Meet Again in Kerala!
