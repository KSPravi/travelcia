Magical Munnar Getaway

Discover the rolling hills, misty valleys, and emerald tea gardens of Munnar — Kerala’s most beloved hill station. This short yet soul-refreshing getaway is designed for travelers who wish to unwind amidst nature’s finest beauty.

3 Days / 2 Nights
Day 1 – Arrival in Cochin

Journey to the Hills of Munnar

Welcome to God’s Own Country – Kerala!

Upon arrival at Cochin Airport / Ernakulam Railway Station, our friendly representative will greet you and assist you with your scenic drive to Munnar (approx. 4 hours).

En route highlights:

  • Cheeyappara & Valara Waterfalls – Perfect photo spots!
  • Tea plantations & mist-covered valleys
  • Spice gardens with the aroma of cardamom & pepper

Check in to your resort in Munnar, where you’ll be surrounded by misty mountains and serene tea gardens. Spend the evening relaxing or exploring the local markets.

✨ Overnight stay in Munnar
Day 2

Explore the Beauty of Munnar

Wake up to the scent of fresh mountain air and a delicious breakfast at your resort. Get ready for a full day of sightseeing around Munnar’s best attractions.

Top Attractions:

  • Eravikulam National Park – Home to the rare Nilgiri Tahr and breathtaking rolling grasslands
  • Mattupetty Dam – A perfect spot for boating and photography
  • Echo Point – Shout out and hear nature reply!
  • Kundala Lake – Enjoy a serene pedal boat ride
  • Tea Museum – Learn the art of tea making
  • Rose Garden & Blossom Park – A splash of color amidst the hills

Return to your resort in the evening for a cozy night stay.

✨ Overnight stay in Munnar
Day 3 - Farewell Munnar

Return to Cochin

After breakfast, check out and bid goodbye to the misty hills of Munnar. Drive back to Cochin, cherishing the unforgettable moments of your trip.

If time permits, enjoy a short Cochin city tour covering:

  • Fort Kochi
  • Chinese Fishing Nets
  • Marine Drive

Our representative will drop you at Cochin Airport / Railway Station for your onward journey — taking home memories of serene hills and endless tea gardens.

End of Tour
