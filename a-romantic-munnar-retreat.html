Cherished in the Clouds

A Romantic Munnar Retreat

2 Days / 1 Night
Optional Romantic Add-ons
  • Candlelight Dinner under the stars (on request)
  • Room Decoration with flowers, candles & honeymoon setup (on request)
  • Overnight stay in Munnar – where love lingers in every cloud
Day 1 – Arrival in Cochin

Journey to the
Misty Hills of Munnar

Welcome to God’s Own Country! Upon arrival at Cochin Airport / Ernakulam Railway Station, you’ll be greeted with a warm smile by our representative. Begin your scenic drive to Munnar (approx. 4 hours) — a dreamy hill station that feels like it was painted just for lovers.

As you ascend the winding roads, breathe in the fresh mountain air and let the emerald tea gardens, silvery waterfalls like Cheeyappara and Valara Falls, and mist-kissed valleys set the mood for romance.

On arrival in Munnar, check in to your cozy honeymoon suite or romantic resort, surrounded by whispering trees and rolling hills. Spend your evening hand-in-hand exploring the local market, sipping on freshly brewed tea, or simply relaxing together as the sun sets over the misty mountains.

Day 2 – Return to Cochin

Romantic
Munnar Sightseeing

Wake up to the aroma of tea and the gentle song of the hills. After a delicious breakfast, set out to explore the romantic side of Munnar. Visit the serene Mattupetty Dam, the glassy Kundala Lake (perfect for a couple’s boating experience), and the playful Echo Point, where your laughter echoes back through the hills.

Later, visit the Eravikulam National Park (Rajamalai) — a lush haven of greenery and home to the rare Nilgiri Tahr, offering breathtaking views of the mist-wrapped valleys and endless tea gardens.

After sightseeing, check out and drive back to Cochin (approx. 4 hours), cherishing the sweet silence of the hills and the warmth of your shared memories.

End of Tour
